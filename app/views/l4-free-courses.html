Free courses for jobs

Hundreds of free courses available for those aged 19 and over, across different sectors, from accounting or construction to courses in early years, digital, catering, adult care and more.

What are free courses for jobs?

Free courses for jobs are flexible courses to fit around your life. They vary in length of time, from a few weeks to a year. 

The courses are designed to help you improve your job opportunities and could lead to earning a higher wage. 

There are over 400 free courses on offer linked to a wide variety of different sectors, including accounting, digital, health and social care, construction, early years, catering, agriculture and more. 

The qualifications are equivalent to an advanced technical certificate or diploma, or A levels. 

Who are they for?

They are for adults aged 19 and over, who are unemployed, earn below the National Living Wage annually, or do not already have a level 3 qualification. 

Courses are free but additional financial support to pay for childcare, travel and other costs may be available when speaking to the course provider. 

Where can I take a free course for jobs? 

Free courses for jobs can take place at a college, at a place of work or online.  

They are offered as:  

  • full-time learning
  • part-time learning
  • online learning
