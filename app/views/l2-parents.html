Parents and carers

I want to support my child with their career.

We know that having conversations about the future can be daunting. 

On this page you’ll find information to build your confidence as a parent or carer, to talk to your child or young person about their future training or career choices. 

You will find resources to help with: 

  • advice on how to support someone making training or job choices 
  • financial support for young people
  • information about SEND

Help and support talking to your child

Talking futures is a resource created to help you have informed and constructive conversations with your child about their future. 

Financial help to support your child’s choices

You can apply for an Advanced Learner Loan to help with the costs of a course at a college or training provider in England.  

Support for children with additional needs

Find information and support if you have a child with special educational needs and disabilities.  

Your local council may be able to provide support with additional costs of caring for a child with disabilities.
