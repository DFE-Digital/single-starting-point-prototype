The Skills Toolkit

Flexible courses open to everyone to boost your skills at your own pace in digital, numeracy and transferable skills.

What is the Skills Toolkit?

The Skills Toolkit are free online courses which range from quick 20-minute refreshers to longer courses of a few weeks.  

All the courses are online and flexible, so you can learn around your other commitments and at your own pace.

Subjects include:

  • Digital skills – from getting to grips with using computers to more advanced courses in digital marketing, coding, or computer science
  • Numeracy skills – including practical maths or introductions to bookkeeping
  • Transferable skills – from presentation or leadership skills to courses on resilience and wellbeing 

Digital, numeracy and transferable skills, like communicating confidently or handling stressful situations, are some of the most sought-after by employers.  

Using the Skills Toolkit could help you build up your skills, boost your CV and progress in work.  

Who is the Skills Toolkit for?

The skills toolkit is free and available to all ages whether:

  • you’re out of work
  • thinking about changing jobs 
  • have just graduated
  • keen to keep your mind healthy and busy

Where can I do a Skills Toolkit course? 

All the courses are online and flexible, so you can learn around your other commitments and at your own pace.
