Multiply

Improving your numeracy could help you in your everyday life, from budgeting money to finding a new job. 

What is Multiply?

Multiply are numeracy courses to help adults improve their numeracy skills. They build your confidence with numbers and help put maths into every day, relatable, context. 

They could help you unlock job opportunities or with everyday life, such as helping children with homework and budgeting money.

Multiply offers:

  • free short courses
  • ranging from beginner to advanced
  • covering a wide range of numeracy skills, including: GCSE Maths and Functional Skills Qualifications 

They are flexible so you will be able to learn at a pace that suits you and help you gain a qualification at the end.  

Who is Multiply for?

For adults 19 and over and don’t have maths GCSE at grade C (or equivalent).  

They vary in course length but are free and funded by the government.  

Where can I take a Multiply course?

The courses are classroom based, taking place at a college and include part-time and evening options.
