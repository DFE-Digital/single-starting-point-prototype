Aged 14-19 and not sure what to do next?

Taking your next step in life can feel like a big leap. It’s okay not to have your life mapped out yet! Thinking about your next steps in education and training is a great place to start. A new #GetTheJump campaign has been launched by the Government to help young people do just that.

See which training and education choices are out there for you, all in one place. Learn more about T Levels, which mix classroom and on-the-job training so you can get a feel of a workplace. Or an Apprenticeship, so you can earn and learn at the same time. Or there are Higher Technical Qualifications which are teaching all the new skills that employers are looking for. The National Careers Service website even includes job profiles to help you discover what different jobs involve. Get the Jump on your choices and you’ll be clued up on everything you need to know to take your next step.
